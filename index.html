About

Hi, I'm Sean Greyvenstein, born and raised in Cape Town, South Africa. I have developed a strong passion for sports and fitness, showing others how I see the world through photography and a love for the outdoors in general. After school, I studied Business Management and Entrepreneurship and went on to study Personal Training to incorporate some of my hobbies into my work environment. I believe that you should never stop learning, always set goals, and follow your interests. I currently own a company that focuses on Earthworks, Paving and Machine hire which has taught me invaluable skills over the years. I have been interested in computers and technology my entire life. I went to "future kids" at age 10 to learn the basics and have loved it ever since. Personally, the aspect of being able to program and create using code and a computer is incredible and the constant evolution of the industry always keeps it interesting.
